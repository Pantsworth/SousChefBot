Grandma's Holiday Apple Pie

 By Carol Smith Published: November 5, 2009 This is my grandmother's apple pie recipe. I like to add a dash of nutmeg. 4.0 stars based on 35 reviews Prep time: 30 min Cook time: 1 hour Total time: 1 hour 30 min Yield: 1 9" pie (8 servings) Serving size: 1 medium slice Calories per serving: 250 Fat per serving: 12g Ingredients: Thinly-sliced apples: 6 cups White sugar: 3/4 cup 1 cup flour Directions: 1. Cut and peel apples. 2. Mix sugar and cinnamon. Use additional sugar for tart apples. 3. Bake pie in oven.
