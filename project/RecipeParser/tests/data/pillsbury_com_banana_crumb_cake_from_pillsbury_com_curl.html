  • 1 Heat oven to 375Â°F. Spray 9 or 8-inch square pan with nonstick cooking spray. In large bowl, combine all cake ingredients; stir 50 to 75 strokes with spoon until mix is moistened. Pour into sprayed pan.
  • 2 In medium bowl, combine all crumb topping ingredients; mix well. Drop by teaspoonfuls over batter.
